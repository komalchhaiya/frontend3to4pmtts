what is URL ?
 URL stands for universal Resource Locator
 for open any website we write its URL.

 types of URL 

 a) absolute URL 
     examples : https://www.flipkart.com
 b) relative url
    examples : https://www.flipkart.com/viewcart?exploreMode=true&preference=FLIPKART
